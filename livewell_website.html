About Us

Care that feels
like family.

Live Well Home Care is a Connecticut-registered home care agency operating under the state's Adult Family Living (AFL) program. We believe the best care comes from those who already love you — and we're here to make that care official, supported, and sustainable.

Our model is simple: we connect eligible clients with family members or loved ones who already live with them, provide training and agency support, and ensure the caregiver is fairly compensated through Medicaid or private pay — so families don't have to choose between love and livelihood.

The Process

Simple steps to peace of mind.

Getting started with Live Well Home Care is straightforward. We guide your family every step of the way — from first call to first care day.

1

Reach Out

Contact us by phone or form. We'll have a warm, no-pressure conversation about your family's situation and needs.

2

Assessment

We coordinate a care needs assessment to determine eligibility and the appropriate level of support (Medicaid or private pay).

3

Caregiver Enrollment

Your family's designated caregiver completes our training program and is officially enrolled — and compensated — through our agency.

4

Care Begins

Services start, the caregiver is supported, and your loved one lives well — at home, where they belong.
